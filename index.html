🚨 THE DUCK SITUATION — READ THIS CAREFULLY 🚨
I am absolutely, obsessively, cosmically in love with ducks. When I see one, I will chase it with my entire soul and completely forget you exist. I will run. And run. And run. I do not stop. I do not look back. You are no longer real to me. Only the duck is real.

⚠️ Keep me on a tight leash near any water, pond, park, or anywhere a duck might reasonably exist. I am not responsible for my actions once the duck appears. This is not a negotiation.

My Grooming Schedule
I am a distinguished gentleman and I require maintenance.
👂
Ear Cleaning — Once a week (or 2-3x if I've been in water)
My ears are long and magnificent and they collect everything. Clean them weekly. If I've been near water — which, given the duck situation, is possible — bump it up to 2-3 times that week. I will not enjoy this but I will endure it with dignity.
🪥
Teeth Brushing — Every night, gently please
Every single night. Yes, really. I will give you a Look. Brush gently. I have feelings. This is non-negotiable — I have a whole mouth full of teeth that need to last me a lifetime.
💡 Pro tip from me, Coco: I become 40% more cooperative during grooming if you show me a treat first. I am corruptible. Use this knowledge.

On The Topic of Treats
My primary love language and life philosophy

Treats are everything to me. Treats are currency. Treats are love. Want me to sit? I'll sit beautifully. Want me to come inside? I'll sprint. Want me to stop barking at the TV? A treat will not actually stop me, but I'll pause long enough to eat it, which buys you approximately 8 seconds of peace.

I will look at you with the saddest eyes you have ever seen on any living creature, in any country, in all of recorded history, and I will make you believe I have never eaten a single treat in my life. I am lying. Do not give me extra treats. I am full. I am always fine. I am a professional actor.
